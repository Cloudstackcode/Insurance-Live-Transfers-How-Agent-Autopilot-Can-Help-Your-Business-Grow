What Are Insurance Live Transfers?

Insurance live transfers are an efficient way for insurance companies to directly connect with potential clients who have already expressed interest in a product or service. In this process, a lead is generated through marketing campaigns, such as digital ads or telemarketing, and then transferred to an agent while the lead is still on the phone. This real-time connection eliminates the delays of traditional lead nurturing and allows agents to engage with customers immediately.

The key advantage of live transfers is that they help ensure a higher level of engagement with leads who are actively interested in learning more about insurance products. As a result, your team spends less time qualifying leads and more time closing deals.
